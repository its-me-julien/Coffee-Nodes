About us World Mobile

World Mobile

Connecting the Unconnected

World Mobile is connecting the unconnected around the globe. While traditional networks have left 3.5 billion people without access, World Mobile is brewing a revolution.

Powered by Nodes

The network operates through two essential components: EarthNodes and AirNodes. EarthNodes are the network's brain, processing transactions and managing digital identities through blockchain technology. AirNodes are the physical infrastructure that provides actual internet connectivity, operated by community members.

Community-Owned Network

This combination creates a network that's owned and operated by people rather than corporations. By putting connectivity into community hands, World Mobile delivers affordable, sustainable internet access to areas that traditional telecoms have ignored.

About us

We're a team of blockchain enthusiasts and connectivity pioneers, driven by the belief that internet access should be universal. As Earth Node operators on the World Mobile Chain, we maintain critical infrastructure that powers this revolutionary network.
